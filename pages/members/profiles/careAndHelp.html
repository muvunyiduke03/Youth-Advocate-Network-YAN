About

In the heart of Rwanda, Care and Help Child Organization, founded in 2018, stands as a beacon of hope for vulnerable children. Born from a single act of profound compassion, this organization is dedicated to breaking barriers to education, ensuring safety, fostering mental well-being, and empowering young minds to build brighter, more equitable futures.

Care and Help Child Organization's mission is clear and impactful: to break barriers to education, empower underprivileged children, and guide them toward brighter, more equitable futures. By forging strong partnerships with parents and communities, they strive to reach children who have dropped out of school, provide crucial support for continuous learning during vacations, and ensure that no child is left behind. Their advocacy goal is equally comprehensive, revolving around the holistic education of children, encompassing formal learning, the fundamental right to a life free from violence, and the nurturing of good mental health and sexual and reproductive health (SRH).
