About

Born from the ashes of a nation's painful past, Rwanda We Want Organization (RWW), founded in 2015, stands as a testament to resilience and hope. This youth-driven organization is passionately committed to empowering Rwandan youth, equipping them with the leadership skills, support systems, and opportunities they need to drive sustainable development and build the prosperous Rwanda they envision.


Rwanda We Want Organization operates with a clear and impactful mission: to empower Rwandan youth through comprehensive programs focused on leadership development, advocacy, mental well- being, and enhanced livelihoods, ultimately enabling them to be the driving force behind sustainable development. Their vision is of a prosperous Rwanda, a nation where empowered youth are at the forefront, leading the way towards a brighter future. Operating in Bugesera, Nyanza, and Ngoma, RWW is making significant strides in realizing this vision.
