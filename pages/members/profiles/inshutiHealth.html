About

In the heart of Nyagatare District, Inshuti Health Organization (IHO), founded on a powerful vision of health equity and empowerment, is making tangible strides in transforming lives. This dedicated organization is committed to providing comprehensive health education, advocating for crucial rights, and fostering economic opportunities, building healthier and more resilient communities where every individual has the chance to thrive.


Inshuti Health Organization envisions a future where every individual in Rwanda enjoys equitable access to quality healthcare, comprehensive health education, and empowering economic opportunities, leading to healthier and more resilient communities. Their mission is the driving force behind this vision: to ensure the provision of equitable health services, strengthen community-led health systems, and actively advocate for policies that improve the overall well-being of underserved populations across Rwanda.
