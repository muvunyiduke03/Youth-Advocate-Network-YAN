About

In the heart of Rwanda, a dynamic, youth-led organization is rising to meet the urgent health needs of marginalized and impoverished communities. OAZIS Health, founded in 2020, is not just addressing health challenges; they are building bridges of access, knowledge, and empowerment, ensuring that even the most vulnerable have an equal opportunity to live healthy, dignified, and fulfilling lives.


OAZIS Health's vision is bold and aspirational: a world where everyone, everywhere, has an equal opportunity to live a healthy, dignified, and fulfilling life. Their mission is the driving force behind this vision: to save lives and improve health for those who need it most by actively promoting equity and access for all. Based in Bugesera (Nyamata), this grassroots organization is demonstrating the power of youth-led innovation in tackling complex health challenges.
