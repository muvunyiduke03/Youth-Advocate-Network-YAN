About

MINDORA HEALTH, established in 2022, is pioneering a transformative approach to youth mental healthcare. By harnessing the power of AI-driven digital innovations and an engaging Gameboard, MINDORA HEALTH is predicting, detecting, monitoring, and assessing mental health conditions, making quality support accessible, affordable, and stigma-free for adolescents and youth across the nation, with a strong focus on reaching underserved communities.


MINDORA HEALTH envisions a future where a mentally resilient generation thrives across Rwanda and Africa. To achieve this ambitious vision, their mission is to strategically harness the power of AI-powered digital health innovations and their unique Mindora AI Gameboard. This technology is employed to proactively predict, accurately detect, continuously monitor, and comprehensively assess a range of mental health conditions prevalent among youth, including Depression, Anxiety, PTSD, Bipolar Disorder, and ASUD. By leveraging these cutting-edge tools, MINDORA HEALTH ensures that young people receive timely early intervention and consistent, ongoing support, fostering a culture of proactive mental well-being.
