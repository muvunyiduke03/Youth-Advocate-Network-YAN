About

Hope for Tomorrow is a youth-led nonprofit working to break the barriers that keep rural girls from reaching their full potential, especially in education and reproductive health. The organization is based in Nyamasheke District, Rwanda, and provides scholarships, capacity building & leadership development, and SRHR support to youth by establishing clubs in schools and organizing community sessions for out-of-school youth. These safe spaces equip youth with knowledge about Sexual and reproductive health, as well as leadership skills they need to thrive and reach their full potential.


Since 2024 when Hope for Tomorrow started, it has returned to school 20 girls on full scholarships, created three SRH clubs in different schools, and reached over 300 adolescents through weekly GSRH sessions. The organization provides menstrual products to support girls to stay in school by reducing school absenteeism. In this line, we launched a menstrual cup pilot with young women, which is going to bring a sustainable solution to period poverty.
