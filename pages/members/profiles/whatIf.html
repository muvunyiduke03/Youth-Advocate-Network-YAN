About

In the heart of Rwanda, where resilience blooms from the soil of history, a powerful movement is taking root. WHAT IF-Rwanda isn't just an organization; it's a lifeline of love and mentorship, nurturing vulnerable children and painting vibrant futures where once there was only uncertainty.

For many children at the Iramiro Center, the concept of family has been tragically redefined by loss and hardship. But amidst these challenges, a beacon of hope shines brightly – the dedicated mentors of WHAT IF-Rwanda. Stepping into the roles of 'aunties' and 'uncles' these compassionate individuals are forging profound and enduring bonds, offering the unwavering support that every child deserves.
