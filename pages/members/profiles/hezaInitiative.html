About

In the heart of Rwanda, a youth-led organization, Heza Initiative, founded in 2022, is passionately committed to transforming lives from the ground up. With a focus on improved nutrition and health, Heza Initiative is empowering communities – particularly women, children, and vulnerable groups – to overcome malnutrition and build sustainable pathways to a healthier future.


Heza Initiative envisions a Rwanda where maternal and child health thrives, driven by knowledge, skills, and access to essential resources. Their mission is to equip vulnerable women and children with precisely these tools, empowering them to promote their own well-being and build a foundation for a healthier future. Operating in Rulindo and Shyorongi, Heza Initiative is demonstrating the profound impact that targeted, community-driven initiatives can have on tackling malnutrition in all its forms.
