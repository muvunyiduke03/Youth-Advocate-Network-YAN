About

The Nursing Research Club Organization, founded in 2021, is empowering early-career nursing professionals to become impactful researchers and bridging the knowledge gap within communities on critical health issues through evidence-based practices and innovative outreach.


The Nursing Research Club Organization is driven by a powerful vision: to be a leading force in advancing community health through rigorous nursing research, fostering innovation, and championing evidence-based practice. Their mission is to champion health research within the nursing profession, serve as a representative voice for nursing in the community, and conduct impactful research and evidence-based activities that demonstrably improve and sustain public health across Rwanda.
