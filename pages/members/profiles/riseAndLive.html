About

In Rwanda, where young motherhood can often be accompanied by immense challenges, Rise and Live Organization stands as a beacon of hope and empowerment. Founded on a deep understanding of the unique needs of teen mothers, this organization is dedicated to fostering mental resilience, providing crucial knowledge, and equipping these young women to lead fulfilling lives and build brighter futures for themselves and their children.


Rise and Live Organization is a strong advocate for the rights and well-being of teen mothers, focusing on critical areas such as consistent mental health support, comprehensive access to reproductive health education and services, the fundamental right of teen mothers to a second chance and a robust support system, and the creation of meaningful opportunities for these young women to rise above their challenges and truly thrive.
