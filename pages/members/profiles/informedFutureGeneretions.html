About

The mission of Informed Future Generations is clear and vital: to cultivate informed decision-makers among young people by providing comprehensive education and resources on sexual reproductive health and rights, HIV/AIDS, substance abuse, and related mental health challenges. IFG envisions a Rwanda where youth are not only healthy but also empowered to make informed decisions for their lives and the well- being of their communities. Operating across Rwamagana, Gatsibo, Kayonza, Ngoma, and Kirehe, IFG is rapidly establishing itself as a crucial resource for young Rwandans.

Addressing the deeply rooted causes of gender-based violence, teenage pregnancies, and gender inequality, IFG's 'Like Your Sister' program works to challenge harmful social norms and promote a culture of respect and care for girls and women. By fostering a sense of equality and empowerment among young people, this program aims to create a safer and more inclusive society where all youth can thrive, free from violence and discrimination
